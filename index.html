Projects

Data Science Analysis of patients in Healthcare

Worked on deep data and statistical analysis of a healthcare Startup to get insights from patients data and obtain/suggest new treatment improvements. The project was developed in Python, with the main libraries being Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, seaborn, and Matplotlib. Articles were produced and published in international journals, with high-level scientific writing, aiming to corroborate the Startup's medical treatment.

Power Transformers's downtime prediction

Worked in a data science project for modeling the Power Transformers's downtime with some days in advance to the failure event in collaboration with Furnas/Eletrobras. Two indicators of the power transformer were modeled: CAI (Chromatographic Assay Indicator) and EFRI (Electric Failure Risk Indicator). The models were built with Python and TensorFlow and put into production.

Inspection based on Risk

Participated in the whole project of developing Web applications for predicting the likelihood of failure within a time window for pipes in partnership with Petrobras. The project was developed in Python (Django) and JavaScript, React, Bootstrap and jQuery, with the front-end developed mainly in Javascript and the back-end in Django. The project is under development.
